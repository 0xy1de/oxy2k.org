Overview

LinX OS is a retro‑inspired Linux distribution designed to capture the look and feel of early‑2000s desktops while offering modern security and drivers. Built on the Oxyde Desktop environment, LinX OS blends Aqua‑style glass with Frutiger Aero aesthetics.

  • Lightweight and efficient (target < 1 GB RAM with Oxyde).
  • Familiar table‑based visuals, nostalgic UI details.
  • Modern kernel support with retro theming.
